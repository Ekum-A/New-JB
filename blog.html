How to Write a Job-Winning Resume

A great resume grabs attention in seconds. Here are key tips:

  • Tailor it: Customize for the specific job you're applying for.
  • Use bullet points: Keep it clear and concise.
  • Highlight achievements: Not just responsibilities.
  • Keep it clean: Use easy-to-read fonts and consistent formatting.

Keep your resume ideally one page long unless you have 10+ years of experience. Always include a link to your portfolio or LinkedIn if possible.

Top 10 Interview Questions & Answers
  1. Tell me about yourself: Give a short summary of your professional background.
  2. Why do you want this job? Connect your skills to the job description.
  3. What are your strengths? Choose 2–3 relevant to the role.
  4. What’s your greatest weakness? Be honest but show how you're improving it.
  5. Tell me about a challenge and how you handled it.
  6. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
  7. Why should we hire you?
  8. Tell me about a time you worked in a team.
  9. How do you handle pressure?
  10. Do you have any questions for us?

Practice answers using the STAR method: Situation, Task, Action, Result.

Finding Legit Remote Work in 2025

Remote work is booming—but beware of scams. Here are tips for landing legit remote jobs:

  • Use trusted sites like Upwork, LinkedIn, FlexJobs, and Remote OK.
  • Never pay upfront for job offers.
  • Build a professional LinkedIn profile and personal brand.
  • Keep your portfolio up to date with samples of your work.

Bonus tip: Set up a productive work environment at home to stay efficient and focused.
